Ethical AI Framework

The C.O.A.C.H. Framework

Tap each letter to reveal what it stands for and how it applies to ethical AI use in Peer Coaching.
C
Clarity ▾
Ensure inputs and outputs are clear, accurate, and understandable; avoid misleading or ambiguous information.
O
Ownership ▾
Take responsibility for how AI is used, including verifying results and being accountable for decisions.
A
Alignment ▾
Use AI in ways that align with ethical standards, institutional policies, and intended purposes.
C
Confidentiality ▾
Protect sensitive data and privacy; never input or share restricted or personal information improperly.
H
Human-Centeredness ▾
Prioritize human judgment, well-being, and fairness—AI supports decisions, not replaces responsibility.
